Hobbies

These are some of my favorite hobbies that I enjoy during my free time:

Reading

I have a deep passion for manhwa, manga, and other genres related to these, often exploring unique worlds filled with adventure, fantasy, and compelling character development. I find it fascinating how these stories often blend elements of art and narrative in a way that brings the story to life.

Photography

Photography is more than just a hobby for me; it’s about capturing candid moments that speak volumes. I love taking photos that reflect real, unposed emotions, and the raw beauty of everyday life. Whether it’s a fleeting moment or a grand scene, I feel it's important to capture what is often overlooked.

Cooking

Cooking is something I enjoy when I’m in the mood to try something new or just unwind. I like experimenting with different recipes, exploring various cuisines, and sometimes getting creative with ingredients. Although, to be honest, I often end up feeling too lazy to cook and sometimes opt for simpler meals!

Online Games

When I’m in the mood for some fun and relaxation, online games offer an escape into immersive virtual worlds. I enjoy both competitive and casual games, where I can challenge myself, team up with friends, or just unwind with a great story.
